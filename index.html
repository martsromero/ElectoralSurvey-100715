#PhVote2016

  • Highlights

The survey fieldwork was conducted by Pulse Asia Research, Inc. from September 8 to 14, 2015 using face-to-face interviews.

This nationwide survey is based on a sample of 2,400 representative adults 19 years old and above and has a +/- 2% error margin at the 95% confidence level.

Subnational estimates for Metro Manila, the rest of Luzon, Visayas and Mindanao have a +/- 4% error margin, also at 95% confidence level.

Senator Grace Poe leads the May 2016 presidential race at 26% while second place is shared by three other probable presidential candidates.

Sharing second place are former Department of Interior and Local Government Secretary Manuel A. Roxas II at 20%, Vice President Jojo Binay at 19% and Davao City Mayor Rodrigo Duterte at 16%.
